What?

Libsass is C/C++ port of the Sass engine. The point is to be simple, fast, and easy to integrate. Find out more about the project over at Github.

About

This project is the brain child of Hampton Catlin, the original creator of Sass, and is sponsored by Moovweb. Aaron Leung from Moovweb is the primary developer.

Libsass brought to you by Aaron Leung and Hampton Catlin (@hcatlin)
